About Me

My name is Yusuf Utkan, and I am a student at DePaul University pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a focus on software development.

Why I choose Computer Science?

I chose Computer Science because I enjoy building structured systems, solving problems, and continuously improving how things work. As I continued learning, I realized that I especially enjoy breaking large problems into smaller steps, organizing workflows, and watching projects gradually come together.

What I am doing right now

I am currently focused on building clean, structured, and user-focused applications while strengthening my problem-solving skills and growing as a software developer.

My Journey

Everything started when I was 16 years old. I found a course on YouTube about learning Python. The video promised that I would be able to build cool stuff just by typing code. The hours flew by as I finished the course and entered the world of programming.

My first steps into programming

Although I had a lot to learn, those early experiences made programming exciting for me. The ability to create something from a few lines of code felt almost magical, and it inspired me to keep exploring and improving my skills.

My first real challenges

As I continued learning, I realized that programming was not just about writing code that works. It was also about understanding problems, fixing mistakes, and learning how to think logically. At first, even small errors could feel confusing, but each mistake helped me become more patient and more confident as a programmer.

Building my foundation

After learning the basics of Python, I started exploring web development through HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoyed seeing how each language had a different role. HTML gave structure to the page, CSS made it look alive, and JavaScript added interaction. Learning these tools helped me understand how websites are built from the ground up.

Why I keep learning

What keeps me interested in programming is the feeling of progress. Every new concept I learn gives me another tool to build better projects and solve more interesting problems. I know I still have a long way to go, but I enjoy the process of improving step by step and turning ideas into real working programs.

My Goals

My main goal is to become a skilled software developer who can build useful, clean, and creative applications. I want to keep improving my understanding of programming, not only by learning new technologies, but also by becoming better at solving problems and writing code that is easy to read and maintain.

Growing as a developer

As I continue my education, I want to strengthen my foundation in web development, programming logic, and software design. I know that becoming a good developer takes time, practice, and patience. My goal is to keep building projects that challenge me and help me understand how real applications work.

Building meaningful projects

I want to create projects that are more than just practice exercises. My goal is to build websites and applications that solve real problems, help people, or make everyday tasks easier. Each project gives me a chance to learn something new and improve the way I think as a programmer.

Preparing for the future

In the future, I hope to gain experience through internships, teamwork, and real-world development opportunities. I want to continue learning from other developers, improving my skills, and becoming more confident in my abilities. My long-term goal is to turn my interest in programming into a professional career where I can keep growing and creating.
